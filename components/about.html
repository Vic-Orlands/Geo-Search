    • About Geo-Search

    Geo-Search is a tool that allows people to search for places and extract information such as timezone, map and local weather conditions like temperature, wind speed, humidity, and precipitation. Users can search and view wherever they want from the comfort of their homes.

    Note: This is an Andela Project for those in the foundation course track aimed at equipping and testing their learnt skills so far which is HTML, CSS, Javascript and of course consuming APIs.
